Our New Brewing System
The wait is over! We now have a new brewing system up and running, brewing its first beer!

While the “old” system gave us some delicious beers, it could only brew up to 2 barrels (62 gallons) at a time. With the new system, not only will it produce excellent tasting beers, you are getting more! This new system can brew 4 barrels (124 gallons) – two times more beer coming your way!

The first beer will be ready in time for the Chinese New Year. So, to bring our system good fortune we are holding a contest for our customers to name the first LBC beer out of the new system. It will be a Belgian beer with hints of apricot and cardamom. Submit your creative beer name either via Facebook, email (info@loudounbrewing.com) or in the tasting room by Thursday, January 23 to be considered. The lucky winner will be announced during the beer launch party Saturday, January 25.
